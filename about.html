Hi there! I'm Megan, a student designer currently studying Graphic Design at Illinois State University. I am originally from the suburbs of Chicago, but am currently residing in Bloomington-Normal, Illinois.


In my graphic design work, I like to focus on the idea of growth. I do this by including organic shapes and elements, as well as handwritten text. I enjoy photography, and a lot of my photography work is also focused on the the idea of nature and growth.
